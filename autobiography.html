My Autobiography

Scroll down to explore the journey of my life

Childhood

Born & Raised in Cauayan City

I was born and raised in Cauayan City, Isabela. I grew up in a family that taught me important values such as hard work, perseverance, respect, and faith in God. I was curious about many things and loved learning new ideas and skills, dreaming about what I wanted to become in the future.

Grade 6

My Parents' Separation

One of the most difficult moments in my life happened when my parents decided to separate. At that age, I found it hard to understand why things had to change. The separation brought pain, confusion, and emotional struggles that affected me deeply, but I learned to adjust to a new reality while continuing my studies.

High School

Building Independence

As I entered high school, I learned to become more independent and resilient. I focused on my education, built meaningful friendships, and worked hard to overcome challenges. During these years, I also developed a growing interest in technology and computers, which inspired me to pursue a career in Information Technology.

Grade 11

Losing My Father

Another heartbreaking event occurred when my father passed away. Losing a parent is one of the most painful experiences a person can go through. His death left a deep wound in my heart, but through those difficult times, I learned the importance of faith, perseverance, and appreciating the people who remain by our side.

College

BSIT at ISU Cauayan

Today, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) at Isabela State University — Cauayan Campus. I continue to improve my programming, technical, and problem-solving skills. I actively participate in academic activities, competitions, and student organizations that help me grow both professionally and personally.

Faith

Strength Through God

My faith in God has been one of the greatest sources of strength throughout my journey. During times of pain, loss, and uncertainty, I found comfort and hope in my relationship with Him. My faith has taught me to trust God's plan, even when I do not fully understand it, and to keep moving forward despite life's difficulties.

The Future

My Goal as an IT Professional

My goal is to become a successful Information Technology professional who can use technology to make a positive impact on society. Through hard work, faith, and perseverance, I believe it is possible to overcome challenges and achieve meaningful goals. My journey has been marked by both joy and pain, but every experience has contributed to who I am today.
